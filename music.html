My Passion

In 2010...

I fell in love with electronic music. The first song I heard was Deadmau5's Strobe and from there I was hooked. I slowly began listening to the subgenres of trance, electro and trap. After about two years of listening, I needed something more. After attending my first music festival in 2012, I decided it was time to give DJing a shot.
